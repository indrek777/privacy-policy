Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: On which devices can I play Pixel Skies?
A: Pixel Skies is designed for iPhone and iPad devices running iOS 11 or later.

Q: How do I mute or unmute the sound?
A: Tap the music icon on the main screen to toggle sound on or off.

Q: Is my progress or best score saved?
A: Your best score is saved locally on your device.

Q: Does the game require an internet connection?
A: No, Pixel Skies can be played offline. An internet connection may be used only for anonymous analytics and crash reporting.
