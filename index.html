1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Inreco ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal data when you use the Events 4u application (the "App").

If you have any questions, please contact us using the details in the Contact section.

The data we collect depends on your usage and the permissions you grant. Use the list below to reflect your App’s actual behavior (remove items that do not apply):

Contact information

Name, email address, phone number. if applicable

Account information

Username, profile details, authentication data. if applicable

Payment information

Purchases and subscriptions are processed securely by payment processors (e.g., Apple In‑App Purchases). We do not store card numbers.

3. How we use data

  • To provide App functionality and deliver services
  • To provide customer support and communicate with you
  • For analytics and to improve the App
  • To prevent fraud and ensure security
  • To comply with legal obligations

Legal bases (for EU users): consent; contract performance; legitimate interests; legal obligation.

4. Data sharing

We may share data with the following categories of partners, only for the purposes described in this Policy:

  • Service providers (administration, hosting, cloud, backups)
  • Analytics providers (if used)
  • Payments (Apple In‑App Purchases; we do not store payment card data)
  • Advertising partners (if used; we comply with Apple’s tracking requirements)
  • Legal compliance (where required by law or to protect our rights)

We do not sell your personal data.

5. Data retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described or for the period required by law. When data is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it.

6. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

7. Children’s privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected data from children under this age, we will delete it within a reasonable time.

8. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Request deletion (the "right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict or object to processing

  • Withdraw consent (where processing is based on consent)
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details in the Contact section.
